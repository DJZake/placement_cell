Privacy Policy for Placement Cell Management System

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Placement Cell Management System (PCMS) collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its users. By accessing or using the PCMS, you consent to the collection, storage, and processing of your personal information as described in this policy.

    1. Information We Collect
    • Personal Information: We may collect personal information from users, including but not limited to name, contact information, and academic records.
    • Usage Data: We may collect information about how users interact with the PCMS, including IP addresses, browser types, and operating systems, as well as the pages visited and actions taken within the platform.
    2. How We Use Information
    • To Provide Services: We use the information collected to provide placement services, facilitate communication between users and recruiters, and optimize the functionality of the PCMS
    • To Improve User Experience: We use usage data and feedback from users to improve the design, features, and performance of the PCMS, and to customize content and recommendations based on user preferences.
    3. Information Sharing and Disclosure
    • With Recruiters: Users' personal information may be shared with recruiters for the purpose of job applications, interviews, and recruitment processes. However, users' consent will be obtained before sharing any personal information with recruiters.
    4. Data Security
    • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the security of the system.
    5. Children's Privacy
    • The PCMS is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18.
    6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
    • We reserve the right to update or amend this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes to this policy, and continued use of the PCMS after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
